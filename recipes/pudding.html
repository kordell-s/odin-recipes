Pudding

Description

It's easy to make with basic pantry ingredients and some day-old bread. Delicious for breakfast or dessert with milk poured over the top or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 4 cups bread cubes
  • 1/2 cup raisins (optional)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a small saucepan over low heat, heat milk. Add butter and stir until melted. Let cool to lukewarm.
  2. In a large bowl, combine sugar, bread, raisins, eggs, salt, cinnamon, and vanilla. Slowly pour in the lukewarm milk mixture. Let stand for a few minutes, then pour into a 2-quart baking dish.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes, or until the top springs back when lightly tapped.
